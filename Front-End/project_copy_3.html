The Vinderen house is a multi-generation family house. Everything was designed around the dynamic stages in the life of the family.


Instead of changing the house (and most of the time the neighborhood) we have designed a house that changes and adapts itself to the needs of the user. The classical change of status being as follows:


Couple-

Couple with kids-

Grown-up kids with couple-


Therefore the house has builtin in its plan the flexibility required by all these life stage - changes.
